About Me

After working as an educator for 8 and a half years in low socioeconomic and urban communities, I have a newfound respect for the classroom teacher. Along with curriculum, they teach responsibility, resilience, empathy, productivity and much more. I have seen many qualified, passionate educators lose steam due to the demands of their work. As a web developer, I endeavor to make teaching and learning easier for teachers. Especially within this season of ‘home school learning’, its important that educators have access to distance learning tools that will continue to make their work meaningful. I believe:
  • Everyone’s input is considered valuable | Cultivating a learning culture is paramount | Collaboration is key to the success of the project | When necessary, working remotely is a viable option | Most importantly, the work done is produced with excellence, meeting client expectations, with the sole intent of contributing positive product to bridge socioeconomic gaps.
